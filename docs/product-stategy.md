# Product Strategy Logic

## Two Types of Opportunities

### 1. Distribution Opportunities
- Existing products entering market
- Example:
  - Tempeh chips
  - Trending beverages

### 2. Product Development Opportunities
- Build new PoP-branded products

## Strategy Pattern
Trend → Ingredient → Product

Examples:
- Adaptogens → ginseng snack
- Kombucha → ginger kombucha
- Gut health → herbal tea blend

## Key Insight
PoP should not just follow trends —
it should adapt them into its core strengths.
