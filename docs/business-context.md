# Business Context: Prince of Peace (PoP)

## What PoP Does
- Imports and distributes ~800 products
- Supplies 100,000+ retail outlets
- Connects global suppliers to U.S. shelves :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Key Challenge
PoP is:
- Data-rich
- Insight-poor

Problems:
- No API systems
- No real-time data pipelines
- Buyers rely on spreadsheets

## Buying Team
- 5 buyers managing ~1000 SKUs manually

## Key Insight
Future goal:
> Systems recommend decisions, buyers approve them

## Market Complexity
Two divisions:
1. American retail (structured, predictable)
2. Asian markets (fast-moving, opportunistic)

## Key Advantage
- Deep expertise in:
  - Ginger
  - Ginseng
- Strong access to Asian markets

This allows:
- Unique product development opportunities
- Early access to trends outside the U.S.
