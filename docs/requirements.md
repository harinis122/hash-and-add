# System Requirements

## Core Discovery
The system must:
- Ingest data from at least 2 public sources
- Identify trending:
  - Products
  - Ingredients
  - Categories
- Rank opportunities based on signal strength

## Business Filtering
All outputs must pass:

### Shelf Life
- Must exceed 12 months

### FDA Compliance
- No restricted ingredients

### Trade Risk
- Flag high tariff / restricted countries

### Categorization
Map into:
- Dry goods
- Confections
- Teas
- Personal care
- Health & wellness

## Product Development Layer
System should also:
- Suggest new product ideas using trends

Examples:
- Kombucha trend → ginger kombucha
- Adaptogens → ginseng functional snack :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Output Requirements
- Must be understandable by non-technical users
- Must include:
  - What the trend is
  - Why it matters
  - Recommended action
